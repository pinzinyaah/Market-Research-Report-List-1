<p><ul><li>Single-stage</li><li>Multistage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radial flow turbine market is divided into two main types: single-stage and multistage turbines. Single-stage turbines have one rotor and are generally simpler and more compact, making them suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, multistage turbines consist of multiple rotors arranged in series, allowing for higher pressure ratios and efficiency, ideal for large-scale industrial applications. The selection between these types depends on the specific energy and performance requirements of the application and the operational environment.</p></p>
