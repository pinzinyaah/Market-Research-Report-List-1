<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC gas monitor market encompasses various sensor technologies, primarily including Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID monitors utilize ultraviolet light to ionize gases, offering high sensitivity for detecting volatile organic compounds, making them suitable for industrial applications. On the other hand, MOS sensors rely on semiconducting materials that change resistance when exposed to VOCs, providing a cost-effective solution for indoor air quality monitoring. Both technologies cater to diverse sectors such as environmental, industrial, and safety monitoring.</p></p>
