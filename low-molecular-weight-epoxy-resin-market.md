<p><p>The low molecular weight epoxy resin market is categorized primarily into BPA type, BPF type, and others. BPA (Bisphenol A) type epoxy resins are derived from phenol and acetone, offering excellent adhesion and mechanical properties, widely used in coatings and adhesives. BPF (Bisphenol F) type resins, on the other hand, provide enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability, making them suitable for industrial applications. The "others" category encompasses alternative formulations, catering to specialized needs across various industries like construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
