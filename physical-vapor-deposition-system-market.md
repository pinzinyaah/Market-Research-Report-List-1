<p><p>The Physical Vapor Deposition (PVD) system market comprises various technologies: </p><p>1. **Cathodic Arc Vapor Deposition** involves an electric arc to vaporize metal, creating a dense coating.</p><p>2. **Electron Beam Vapor Deposition** uses focused electron beams to evaporate materials for thin films.</p><p>3. **Sputter Deposition** ejects atoms from a target material using plasma, depositing them on a substrate.</p><p>4. **Ion Implantation** involves bombarding surfaces with ions to modify properties.</p><p>5. **Pulsed Laser Vapor Deposition** employs high-intensity laser pulses to vaporize materials for diverse applications.</p></p>
