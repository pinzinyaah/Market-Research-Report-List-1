<p><p>Carbon Fiber Reinforced Plastic (CFRP) is a composite material known for its high strength-to-weight ratio and rigidity, making it ideal for various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and construction. The market for CFRP is divided into two main types: thermosetting CFRP and thermoplastic CFRP. Thermosetting CFRP involves resins that irreversibly harden and provide excellent thermal and chemical resistance, while thermoplastic CFRP uses reversible resins that allow for easier recycling and processing, enabling rapid production and reusability in manufacturing.</p></p>
