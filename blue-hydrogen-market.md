<p><p>Blue hydrogen is produced through methods like Steam Methane Reforming (SMR) and Auto Thermal Reforming (ATR), both of which convert natural gas into hydrogen while capturing carbon emissions. SMR involves reacting methane with steam to produce hydrogen and carbon dioxide, while ATR combines methane with oxygen and steam for a more efficient process. Both methods are pivotal in the blue hydrogen market, enabling a cleaner hydrogen production pathway by reducing greenhouse gas emissions associated with traditional hydrogen production methods.</p></p>
