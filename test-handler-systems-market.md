<p><ul><li>Gravity-Feed Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li><li>Pick-and-Place Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Test handler systems are specialized equipment used in the semiconductor industry for testing integrated circuits. </p><p>Gravity-feed handlers utilize gravity to position devices for efficient testing, ensuring a streamlined process with minimal complexity. </p><p>Turret handlers feature rotating platforms that enable the simultaneous testing of multiple devices, enhancing throughput and efficiency. </p><p>Pick-and-place handlers use robotic arms to precisely position devices for testing, providing flexibility and adaptability for different test configurations and requirements. Together, these handlers cater to diverse testing needs in the semiconductor market.</p></p>
