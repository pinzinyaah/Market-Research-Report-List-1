<p><strong>The Current Sense Amplifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-side Sensing</li><li>Low-side Sensing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Current Sense Amplifier market is categorized into high-side and low-side sensing types, each serving distinct applications. High-side sensing monitors current directly from the supply line, offering better voltage measurement and improved safety by detecting faults before they impact the load. Low-side sensing, situated between the load and ground, is simpler and less expensive but may be less effective for certain safety-critical applications. Both types are crucial for efficient power management in electric vehicles, renewable energy systems, and industrial applications.</p></p>
