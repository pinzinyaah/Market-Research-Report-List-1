<p><p>The secure USB drives market is divided into two main types: software-based and hardware-based secure USB drives. Software-based secure USB drives utilize encryption software to protect data, often requiring password authentication and offering flexibility in deployment. In contrast, hardware-based secure USB drives incorporate built-in security features, such as encryption chips and biometric access, providing enhanced protection against unauthorized access and physical tampering. Both types cater to different user needs, with hardware-based drives typically offering superior security levels.</p></p>
