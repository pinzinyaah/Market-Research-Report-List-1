<p><strong>The Night Vision Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Image Intensifier</li><li>Thermal Image</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The night vision equipment market primarily consists of two types: image intensifier systems and thermal imaging devices. Image intensifiers amplify low light levels to produce visible images, making them ideal for observing in darkness with some ambient light. In contrast, thermal imaging detects heat emitted by objects, enabling visibility in complete darkness and through obscurants like smoke. Both technologies find applications across military, law enforcement, and civilian sectors, enhancing situational awareness and safety during night operations.</p></p>
