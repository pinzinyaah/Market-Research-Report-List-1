<p><p>The cementitious grout market is primarily divided into two types: General Purpose and High Performance. General Purpose grout is designed for standard applications, providing adequate strength and durability for tasks like filling voids and anchoring. In contrast, High Performance grout offers enhanced properties, such as superior compressive strength, reduced shrinkage, and improved resistance to harsh environments. This makes it suitable for specialized applications in industrial, civil, and structural projects where greater reliability and longevity are essential.</p></p>
