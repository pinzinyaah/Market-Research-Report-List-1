<p><p>The organic dispersants market is primarily categorized into two types: oligomer organic dispersants and polymer organic dispersants. Oligomer organic dispersants are low molecular weight compounds that enhance the distribution of pigments or additives in various formulations, improving stability and performance. In contrast, polymer organic dispersants are larger, more complex structures that provide superior dispersion capabilities and longer-lasting stability in coatings, inks, and plastics. Both types are essential for optimizing product performance in several industrial applications.</p></p>
